## Featured Projects

<details>
<summary><b>🔗 LinkedIn Lead Engine — 100% Network Growth, Zero Platform Violations</b></summary>

<br/>

**Problem:** B2B sales teams hit LinkedIn's ~1,500 connection ceiling doing manual prospecting, while automation attempts risk account bans.

**Built:** A 3-workflow system that scrapes engagement from competitor posts, filters to decision-makers only (CEO/Founder/Owner), deduplicates globally across all campaigns, verifies outreach history via HeyReach API, and routes qualified leads to rate-limited outreach. ChatGPT sentiment analysis instantly sends Calendly links to interested replies. Custom Next.js dashboard for real-time campaign visibility.

**Result:** 1,500 → 3,000+ connections · Thousands of leads processed weekly · 0% duplicate contact rate · Response time: hours → seconds · Zero LinkedIn violations.

`n8n` `Apify` `HeyReach` `Next.js` `TypeScript` `ChatGPT` `PostgreSQL` `Exponential Backoff`

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🎙️ AI Voice Qualification System — 750+ Leads/Month, Running Live</b></summary>

<br/>

**Problem:** Sales teams waste hours manually cold-calling dormant mortgage/credit leads with inconsistent follow-up.

**Built:** AI calling system that pulls dormant leads from CRM, runs outbound + inbound call handling, qualifies on property/mortgage/credit criteria, auto-advances pipeline stages based on call outcomes. Includes double-attempt logic, voicemail detection, and delayed follow-up scheduling.

**Result:** 750+ leads/month processed consistently · Full call logging for compliance · Manual cold-calling effort replaced entirely.

`n8n` `RetellAI` `GoHighLevel` `BN Touch CRM` `PostgreSQL` `Zapier`

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>📱 WhatsApp AI Assistant — 93% Query Auto-Resolution, Multilingual</b></summary>

<br/>

**Problem:** High-volume WhatsApp customer support causes slow replies, missed orders, and poor follow-up — especially across multiple languages.

**Built:** Multilingual WhatsApp Business assistant that handles product queries, creates orders and bills, detects crop disease from photos (image + text input), runs admin broadcasts, and schedules meetings — all without human intervention on routine queries.

**Result:** ~93% of queries auto-resolved · ~70% reduction in manual replies · 24/7 support and sales coverage.

`n8n` `WhatsApp Cloud API` `Python` `Gemini` `ChatGPT` `Groq` `Google Sheets`

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>🎬 UGC Video Ad Generator — Product Image → Authentic Short-Form Video</b></summary>

<br/>

**Problem:** UGC-style video ads convert well but require creators, editing iterations, and budget — hard to scale for e-commerce.

**Built:** Two-mode AI pipeline. Mode 1: analyses product image → generates realistic UGC scene → creates 8-second video. Mode 2: animates between two keyframes with detailed AI-generated transition prompts. Polling + retry logic for reliability at scale.

**Result:** Product brief → campaign-ready UGC video in minutes · Scales creative testing without hiring creators.

`n8n` `GPT-4o` `Claude` `Fal.ai (Nano Banana + Veo 3.1)` `Google Drive`

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>📈 Stock Market Intelligence System — Institution-Style Analysis in Seconds</b></summary>

<br/>

**Problem:** Traders switch between multiple tools for technicals, fundamentals, and news — slowing decisions and losing context.

**Built:** Single webhook that fetches multi-timeframe candlestick data, fundamentals, and recent news in parallel; generates chart images with technical overlays; produces structured recommendations for short-term trades and long-term investing via a dual-LLM pipeline.

**Result:** Full market research compressed to seconds · Consistent risk framing · GPT-4o Mini for sentiment, Claude for final analysis.

`n8n` `TwelveData` `NewsAPI` `Google News RSS` `Chart-IMG` `GPT-4o Mini` `Claude`

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>📣 LinkedIn Content Generation Engine — Research → Write → Approve → Post</b></summary>

<br/>

**Problem:** Consistent, high-quality LinkedIn content requires research, writing, and design time — and most AI-generated content reads like AI.

**Built:** 3-phase engine. Phase 1: scrapes top-performing posts by topic. Phase 2: research agent finds fresh stats and angles, writer agent generates on-brand posts + AI images. Phase 3: Google Sheets approval loop where AI applies human feedback and regenerates images on request.

**Result:** Research-backed, brand-voice-consistent posts with human oversight — scalable content without sacrificing quality.

`n8n` `Apify` `Claude` `Gemini` `GPT-4 Mini` `Google Sheets` `Google Drive`

</details>
